## 2. What Aptiro Is

Aptiro is a personal job-application preparation tool. It helps you organise
your career evidence, tailor application packages, and track opportunities. It
does **not** submit applications, contact employers, or act on your behalf in
any way.

## 3. Permitted Use

You may use Aptiro to:

- Upload and manage your own career content (résumés, profiles, portfolios).
- Build application packages tailored to specific job postings.
- Export those packages for your own use in job applications.
- Track the status of applications you submit yourself.

You may **not** use Aptiro to:

- Fabricate claims about your career history.
- Impersonate another person or entity.
- Submit applications automatically or in bulk.
- Scrape, crawl, or systematically access third-party job sites through Aptiro.
- Reverse-engineer, decompile, or exploit the Service.

## 4. The Non-Negotiables

These behaviours are enforced at the product level and cannot be overridden by
configuration:

### No Auto-Submit

Aptiro **never** submits anything on your behalf. Every status transition in the
application tracker requires explicit human confirmation. Aptiro contains no
mechanism to fill out application forms or click "Submit" on any external site.

### No Fabrication

The AI assist system is constrained to your approved, evidence-backed career
claims only. AI-generated text that introduces metrics, roles, companies, or
other facts absent from your verified career history is **blocked** by the
grounding gate before it is ever shown to you. You are always the final
reviewer.

### No Scraping

LinkedIn, Indeed, and other authentication-walled job boards are explicitly
excluded from all job-import paths. Aptiro does not perform CAPTCHA bypass or
any form of automated login to third-party sites.
